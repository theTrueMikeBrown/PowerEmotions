FAQs 
How much time is required?
I designed this program with busy parents in mind! The training is uploaded in bite-sized chunks you can listen to every morning and implement throughout the day. Or you can watch a week’s worth of information in one sitting and be ready to go. You will need to carve out an hour a week to attend the live call or listen to the replay. Hearing others get coached is almost as valuable as being coached yourself, so listen to the whole call. It is the most transformative part of the program. You’ll also want to hop into the Facebook group daily or near-daily, and post how your work is going a few times a week, but most of us find time for posting on Facebook without much trouble :).
I’ve already read parenting books and bought parenting courses. How is Power Emotions Parenting any different? 
Power Emotions Parenting is NOT a course. It is a coaching program. That means that I am personally invested in your success. It’s not just a bunch of information with a Q&A call where I repeat what was taught in case you missed it. It’s an opportunity to take action and put what you learn into practice, while getting immediate help to lead you through the deep work required to learn new emotional habits. It is structured to take advantage of scientific habit-building strategies so that you achieve a real transformation in your life.
Is Power Emotions a form of attachment parenting/positive parenting/etc? 
The Power Emotions Parenting coaching program is parenting style agnostic. We will work on identifying what parenting style resonates with you for your unique family, and with using your Power Emotions to support you in implementing it consistently and confidently. I may share some techniques that have worked for me, but the program is designed to empower you to choose your own path in parenting.
 
Is everything available immediately? 
No. In order to help you build sustainable habits, you will gain access to a new module each week. The current week and prior weeks will be discussed on the coaching call, and support for anything in the course will be available in the Facebook group.
How long does the program last? 
The coaching program lasts for 8 weeks, to help you get on the path to firm, sustainable habits. You can stay in the private members’ group and get support to keep going for a full YEAR for free in order to really get those habits rock solid. You will keep access to the trainings in the membership site for life.
